The existing repo reports:
- **2,162** prosthetic-related trials (broad search)
- **509** trials focused on prosthetic limb
- US leads with 2,378 site locations
- Zero trials in target regions

| NCT05569967 | Telerehabilitation vs Face-to-Face | Completed | Ankara, Turkey | Telerehab validation |
| NCT06821412 | ASTERISK Wireless Control | Completed | Holliston, MA | EMG pattern recognition |
| NCT05278417 | LIMBER UniLeg 3D Printing | Recruiting (multi-site) | Multiple US sites | 3D-printed prosthetic |

### Key Finding: Evidence-to-Access Disconnect

All identified prosthetic innovation trials are concentrated in major academic medical centers (Boston, Chicago, Houston, Philadelphia). None are in the Appalachian or Deep South regions where amputation rates are highest and care access is most limited. This represents a classic inverse care law: where need is greatest, innovation is least available.

## 3. Orthotic-Specific Gap Analysis

While the original analysis focused on prosthetic devices, orthotic care (braces, ankle-foot orthoses, spinal orthoses) faces the same gap:

- **Zero** orthotist providers in any target region
- **Zero** certified pedorthists (foot orthotics specialists)
- **Zero** spinal orthosis fitting services
- The only related services: podiatry (1 in WV, 0 in KY, 0 in MS Delta) and orthopaedic clinics (1 in MS Delta)

Orthotic needs are particularly urgent in:
- **Eastern KY**: High rates of diabetic Charcot foot require AFO management
- **MS Delta**: Stroke-related hemiplegia requires AFO and KAFO devices
- **Rural WV**: Work-related musculoskeletal injuries require back and limb bracing

## 4. Data Quality Notes

- OSM healthcare data is comprehensive for hospitals and pharmacies but may underrepresent private-practice prosthetists/orthotists
- All searches used ≥50 km radius from region centers
- No state licensure database cross-validation was performed (recommended for future updates)
- Seasonal road conditions in Appalachian regions may increase effective travel distances beyond straight-line calculations
- The Mississippi Delta includes some areas across state lines (AR) where healthcare infrastructure differs
