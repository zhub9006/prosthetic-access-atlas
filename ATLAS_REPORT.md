## Access Gap Analysis

### Rural West Virginia (Marlinton, 38.22N, -80.09W) 🔴 CRITICAL
- No CPO within 50+ miles
- Nearest CPOs: Charleston, WV (~190 mi) / Pittsburgh (~200 mi)
- 14 healthcare facilities found (all non-prosthetic)
- Travel time: 3+ hours
- Medicaid Barrier: WV limits CPO reimbursement

### Eastern Kentucky (Hazard, 37.25N, -83.19W) 🔴 CRITICAL
- No CPO within 100km
- Nearest CPOs: Lexington, KY (~130 mi)
- 19 facilities (pharmacies, general clinics, OT only)
- Travel time: 3+ hours
- Medicaid Barrier: KY Medicaid limited

### Mississippi Delta (Greenville, 33.41N, -91.06W) 🟥 EXTREME
- No CPO within 200+ km
- Nearest CPOs: Memphis, TN (~200 mi) / Jackson, MS (~250 mi)
- 9 facilities (hospital has only 10 beds)
- Travel time: 4+ hours
- Medicaid Barrier: MS NOT expanded
- Highest amputation rates in US from diabetes
