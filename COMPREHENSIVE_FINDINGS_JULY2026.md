### Key Trial Landscape Insights

1. **Only ~17% of limb-amputation prosthetic trials actively recruit** — the pipeline for new evidence is thin.
2. **Phase 3 efficacy trials are critically scarce** — only 2 globally in the amputation-focused subset.
3. **75–76% of studies are observational/registries** — limited interventional device research.
4. **Academic sponsors dominate (77%)** — limits commercial innovation and market translation.
5. **Zero prosthetic clinical trial sites** in WV, KY, or MS, despite high amputation rates.

### Critical Findings

1. **Zero CPO providers** exist within 100 km of any of the six target locations across all three regions.
2. The nearest prosthetic/orthotic provider is **130–200+ miles away**, requiring 3–3.5 hours of driving each way.
3. All three regions are the **highest amputation-rate areas** in the United States.
4. **WV and MS have not expanded Medicaid**; KY has limited expansion — further restricting access.
5. **Zero clinical trial sites** exist in WV, KY, or MS.
