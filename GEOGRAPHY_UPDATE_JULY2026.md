# Geographic Coverage Gap Update — July 2026

## Regions Analyzed

Three underserved regions were selected based on combination of high amputation prevalence, low CPO access, and rural/remote geography:

### 1. Rural West Virginia (Glenville / Sutton area)
- **Coordinates:** 38.93°N, 80.84°W
- **CPO within 30 km:** 0
- **Nearest CPO:** Charleston, WV (~190 mi)
- **Key facilities:** Minnie Hamilton Health System, VA Clinic (Sutton), Care Xpress
- **Medicaid:** Not expanded

### 2. Eastern Kentucky (Pikeville / Williamson area)  
- **Coordinates:** 37.48°N, 82.52°W
- **CPO within 30 km:** 0
- **Nearest CPO:** Lexington, KY (~130 mi)
- **Key facilities:** Logan Foot Clinic, PMC Medical Diagnostics, MCHC Elkhorn City Clinic
- **Medicaid:** Limited expansion

### 3. Mississippi Delta (Clarksdale / Greenville area)
- **Coordinates:** 34.20°N, 90.57°W / 33.41°N, 91.06°W  
- **CPO within 40 km:** 0 (only 1 pharmacy at 20 mi range)
- **Nearest CPO:** Memphis, TN (~200 mi)
- **Key facilities:** None within 40 km radius
- **Medicaid:** Not expanded
- **Note:** Highest amputation rate in the US

## Critical Finding
All three regions have **zero certified prosthetic-orthotic (CPO) providers** within a 30–40 km radius, with the nearest specialist 130–200+ miles away.
