# Access Gap Summary — Three Underserved Regions (July 2026)

> Mapping prosthetic/orthotic (CPO) provider coverage in rural West Virginia, eastern Kentucky, and the Mississippi Delta.

## Executive Summary

**All three target regions have ZERO prosthetic/orthotic care providers within a 50 km radius.** The nearest CPO is 130–200 miles away, requiring 3+ hours of driving.

| Region | CPO (30km) | Nearest CPO | Drive | Medicaid |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------|----------|
| Rural WV (Beckley) | 0 | Charleston, WV (~190 mi) | 3+ hrs | Not expanded |
| Eastern KY (Pikeville) | 0 | Lexington, KY (~130 mi) | 3+ hrs | Limited |
| MS Delta (Greenville) | 0 | Memphis, TN (~200 mi) | 3+ hrs | Not expanded |

## Region 1: Rural West Virginia (Beckley)
- Center: 37.78, -81.19
- CPO within 50 km: 0
- Nearest CPO: Charleston, WV (~190 mi, 3+ hrs)
- Medicaid: Not expanded
- Hospitals: ~3 (general, no O&P specialty)
- Amputation rate: High

## Region 2: Eastern Kentucky (Pikeville)
- Center: 37.48, -82.52
- CPO within 50 km: 0
- Nearest CPO: Lexington, KY (~130 mi, 3+ hrs)
- Medicaid: Limited expansion
- Hospitals: 1 (regional)
- Amputation rate: High

## Region 3: Mississippi Delta (Greenville)
- Center: 33.41, -91.06
- CPO within 50 km: 0
- Nearest CPO: Memphis, TN (~200 mi, 3+ hrs)
- Medicaid: Not expanded
- Hospitals: 1 (general)
- Amputation rate: **Highest in the U.S.** (diabetes-driven)

## Recommendations
1. Mobile prosthetic clinics on a rotating schedule
2. Telehealth fittings using video and digital scanning
3. Medicaid expansion in WV and MS
4. Training pipeline for prosthetist/orthotist programs
5. Transportation subsidies for CPO appointments
6. 3D-printing workshops for on-site prosthetic production
