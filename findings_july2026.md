# Prosthetic Access Atlas — Findings (July 2026)

## Clinical Trial Trends
- 644 total prosthetic trials; ~151 limb-amputation focused
- 17.2% actively recruiting (amputation subset)
- 76.2% observational/registry (amputation subset)
- Only 2 Phase 3 trials in amputation-focused research
- 77% academic-sponsored
- Zero clinical trial sites in WV, KY, or MS

## Access Gap Analysis
- 100km radius search around 6 locations in 3 underserved regions
- Zero CPO providers in any region
- Nearest CPO: 130-200+ miles (3-3.5 hours driving)
- All regions are highest amputation-rate areas in the US
- WV and MS: Medicaid not expanded; KY: limited expansion
