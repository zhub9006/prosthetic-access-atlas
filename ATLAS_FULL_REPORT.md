## Highlighted Clinical Trials

### 1. MIRA — Myoelectric Implantable Recording Array (NCT05768802)
- **Status:** 🟢 RECRUITING
- **Condition:** Amputation; Amputation; Traumatic, Hand
- **Sponsor:** Boninger, Michael, MD
- **Summary:** Evaluates an implantable intramuscular device (MIRA) that senses muscle electrical signals to control a myoelectric prosthesis. Unlike surface electrodes, MIRA is implanted under the skin for improved control. Device stays implanted up to 1 year.
- **Region:** United States

### 2. Powered Ankle Prosthesis with Neural Recording (NCT07645729)
- **Status:** 🟢 RECRUITING
- **Condition:** Amputation
- **Sponsor:** Massachusetts Institute of Technology
- **Summary:** Evaluates a powered ankle prosthesis capable of bidirectional control via neural recording and cutaneous stimulation. Assesses embodiment sense, gait symmetry, and stability during free-space and walking tasks.
- **Region:** United States

### 3. Weight-bearing Training for Trans-tibial Amputees (NCT05095805)
- **Status:** ✅ COMPLETED
- **Condition:** Amputation
- **Sponsor:** University Rehabilitation Institute, Republic of Slovenia
- **Summary:** A pilot RCT using Nintendo Wii Balance Board and COP analysis software for weight-bearing training in trans-tibial amputees receiving their first prosthesis.
- **Region:** Slovenia

### 4. Transdermal Compress Device (NCT06134167)
- **Status:** 🟢 RECRUITING
- **Condition:** Prosthesis and Implants
- **Sponsor:** Balmoral Medical Company
- **Summary:** Evaluates the Transdermal Compress device for participants with transfemoral amputations — a multi-site U.S. study.
- **Region:** United States (9 sites)

### 5. Moisture Management Liner (NCT04427007)
- **Status:** ✅ COMPLETED
- **Condition:** Amputation Stumps, Amputees, Prosthesis User
- **Sponsor:** Liberating Technologies, Inc.
- **Summary:** Tests a silicone liner approach that removes sweat and passively conducts heat using thermally conductive elastomers. Works with the Intrasocket Cooling Element (ICE) device.
- **Region:** United States

### 6. Cyberlegs++ Ortho-prosthesis (NCT03376919)
- **Status:** ❓ UNKNOWN
- **Condition:** Lower Limb Amputation Above Knee (Injury)
- **Sponsor:** Vrije Universiteit Brussel
- **Summary:** Assesses efficacy and usability of the Cyberlegs++ active prosthesis for transfemoral amputees. International project with studies in Belgium and Italy.
- **Region:** Belgium / Italy

### 7. ASSIST — Interim Socket System (NCT05196100)
- **Status:** 🟡 ACTIVE_NOT_RECRUITING
- **Condition:** Amputation
- **Sponsor:** University of Hull
- **Summary:** Explores the acceptability of an interim socket system (Amparo confidence socket) in the immediate rehabilitation of people with transtibial amputation.
- **Region:** United Kingdom

### 8. ITAP — Intraosseous Transcutaneous Amputation Prosthesis (NCT02491424)
- **Status:** ✅ COMPLETED
- **Condition:** Traumatic Amputation of Lower Extremity
- **Sponsor:** Stryker Orthopaedics
- **Summary:** Studies direct skeletal fixation of prosthetic limbs following trans-femoral amputation using the ITAP™ system.
- **Region:** United Kingdom

### 9. Characterizing Ankle Function for Prosthesis Development (NCT01784003)
- **Status:** ✅ COMPLETED
- **Condition:** Traumatic Amputation of Lower Extremity
- **Sponsor:** VA Eastern Colorado Health Care System
- **Summary:** Characterizes biological ankle joint function during sloped locomotion to develop advanced powered ankle-foot prostheses.
- **Region:** United States

## Data Sources & Methodology

1. **ClinicalTrials.gov API** — Searched for condition "prosthesis" (inclusive of amputation, prosthetic interventions). Analyzed 1,491 studies by status, phase, and country.
2. **OpenStreetMap (via Overpass API)** — Searched for healthcare amenities (hospitals, clinics, pharmacies) within 100-30km radius of rural center points.
3. **OSM Routing API** — Calculated driving distances from underserved center points to nearest hospitals.
4. **Github API** — Repository data and structure analysis.

### Limitations
- OSM data may not capture all prosthetic/orthotics providers (some may not be mapped as specialized).
- ClinicalTrials.gov status categories can include "UNKNOWN" which may indicate withdrawn/stalled studies.
- Distance calculations are driving distances, not patient-relevant travel times (may vary with transport availability).
