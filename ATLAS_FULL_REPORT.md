## Key Clinical Trials

### NCT06539936 — Glide Control Strategy for Upper Limb Prostheses

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Status** | 🟢 **RECRUITING** |
| **Start** | Sept 2024 | est. **Completion** | Mar 2027 |
| **Sponsor** | Infinite Biomedical Technologies (Industry) |
| **Collaborators** | NICHD (NIH), Advanced Arm Dynamics |
| **Enrollment** | 7 (estimated) |
| **Phase** | N/A (Device) |
| **FDA Regulated** | Yes (Device) |
| **DMC** | Yes |

**Summary:** Testing the Glide myoelectric control strategy vs. Direct Control. Uses Core2 controller, FlexCell batteries, and novel cursor-based EMG control. Comparisons include CAPPFUL, tBBT, CAPROQ, FIT, DASH, and SAM outcome measures.

**Access Relevance:** Multi-center trial at Arm Dynamics's 6 Centers of Excellence (metro areas). GLIDE technology could improve function for amputees in underserved areas IF it becomes accessible/affordable after regulatory clearance. **No trial sites in rural WV, eastern KY, or Mississippi Delta.**

## Underserved Region Mapping

### 1. Rural West Virginia

**Center:** Beckley, WV (37.78°N, -81.19°W)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Population** | ~93,000 (Raleigh County) |
| **Healthcare Score** | 9.8/10 (Beckley urban core only) |
| **Public Transport** | 0/10 |

**CPO Gap:**
- No CPO facility within 50 miles of rural communities
- Nearest CPO: Charleston, WV (~190 mi; 3.5 hrs)
- WV Medicaid often NOT accepted by CPOs in Charleston/Pittsburgh
- Affected counties: McDowell, Mingo, Boone, Logan, Wyoming
- Amputee exposure: 2-3x national average (diabetes + cardiovascular)

## Coverage Gap Summary

| Region | Healthcare Score | Nearest CPO | Travel Time | Medicaid Barrier | Severity |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|-------------|------------------|----------|
| Rural WV | 9.8 (urban core) / 0 (rural) | ~190 mi | 3-4 hrs | WV Medicaid often declined | 🟠 MOD-HIGH |
| E. Kentucky | **0** | ~130 mi | 3+ hrs | KY accepted, WV not | 🔴 CRITICAL |
| Miss. Delta | **0** | ~200+ mi | 4-5 hrs | No Medicaid expansion | 🔴 CRITICAL |

### Systemic Issues:
1. **Provider deserts:** CPOs cluster in metros. Rural/Delta communities have NONE.
2. **Medicaid barriers:** State-specific failures prevent cross-border access.
3. **Diabetes crisis:** All three regions have diabetes 2-4x national average.
4. **Transportation:** 0/10 on public transport — requires car.
5. **Research relevance gap:** US trials (680 sites) rarely include rural populations.
6. **Industry underinvestment:** Only 16.7% industry-sponsored.

## Recommended Actions

1. **Mobile prosthetics units** for rural WV and E. KY
2. **Teleprosthetics** — virtual consultations for adjustments
3. **MS Medicaid expansion** — highest-impact policy change
4. **CPO incentive programs** — loan repayment for HPSA counties
5. **Community health worker** training in Delta communities
6. **Rural-inclusive clinical trials** — include underserved populations
7. **Industry partnerships** — mandate rural access in regulatory studies
