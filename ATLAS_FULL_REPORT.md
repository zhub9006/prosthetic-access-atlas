### Detailed Region Profiles

#### 1. Rural West Virginia (Beckley, WV)
- **Healthcare score**: 8.1/10
- **Providers found within 20km**: MedExpress (urgent care), Anchor Medical LLC (primary care), CVS Pharmacy, Walgreens, unnamed pharmacy
- **Prosthetic/Orthotic Providers**: **ZERO**
- **Population**: ~800,000 across rural WV
- **Key factors**:
  - WV has one of the highest amputation rates in the U.S. (diabetes + PVD)
  - Mountainous terrain makes travel extremely difficult
  - Limited public transit
  - Many residents lack reliable transportation

#### 2. Eastern Kentucky (Pikeville, KY)
- **Healthcare score**: 8.6/10
- **Providers found within 20km**: PMC Medical Diagnostics, PMC Employee Health, Meta Medical Center (Dr. Parker, DO), Pediatric Associates of Pikeville, Asthma & Allergy Center, Elkhorn Drug, Nichols Apothecary, Big Sandy Dental Center, Akers Family Chiropractic, Dr. Mary Anne Belcher O.D. PSC
- **Prosthetic/Orthotic Providers**: **ZERO**
- **Population**: ~700,000 across eastern KY
- **Key factors**:
  - Among the most economically distressed regions in the U.S.
  - High opioid use disorder rates → amputation risk
  - Remote hollow-topography communities
  - ~25% poverty rate
  - KY has NOT expanded Medicaid

#### 3. Mississippi Delta (Greenville, MS)
- **Healthcare score**: 0/10 — **the lowest possible**
- **Providers found within 20km**: The Greenville Clinic (1 clinic), Michelle Seard-Higgins DMD (dentist), Dental Group of Greenville (dentist)
- **Prosthetic/Orthotic Providers**: **ZERO**
- **Population**: ~500,000 across the Delta
- **Key factors**:
  - One of the most impoverished and medically underserved U.S. regions
  - High diabetes, PVD, and trauma-related amputation rates
  - Predominantly Black, low-income population
  - Many uninsured or underinsured
  - MS has NOT expanded Medicaid
  - No public transit; limited road infrastructure in some areas

## Recommended Interventions

### Immediate (0-6 months)
1. **🚁 Mobile O&P Clinics** — Deploy rotating prosthetic fitting and adjustment vans
2. **📞 Telehealth Prosthetic Consultations** — Store-and-forward socket fitting, remote device adjustments
3. **📋 Provider Registry** — Document all known prosthetists nationwide with wait times and specialties

### Short-Term (6-18 months)
4. **🏥 Hospital Partnerships** — Visiting prosthetist rotations at critical access hospitals
5. **📱 Telemetry-Based Device Adjustment** — Remote microprocessor knee/socket support
6. **🎓 Local Training** — Certify CHWs/technician assistants in basic prosthetic care
7. **💰 Travel Voucher Program** — Assist patients with fuel/hotel costs for distant appointments

### Long-Term (18+ months)
8. **🏛️ Federal Grant Program** — Mandatory prosthetic access funding for HPSAs
9. **🗳️ Policy Reform** — Expand Medicaid in MS and KY; mandate insurance coverage for prosthetic devices
10. **🔬 Research Expansion** — Increase interventional prosthetic trials in underserved regions
