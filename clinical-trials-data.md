### Key Insight
Only ~17% of trials are actively recruiting. The large "UNKNOWN" category (19.9%) signals a transparency gap — many trials lack updated status information, making it harder for patients and clinicians to find relevant studies.

### Key Insight
Nearly 30% of studies have an **Unknown** phase designation, and over 58% have **NA** phase — meaning they are device/surgical studies not classified by standard phase. This makes navigation difficult for patients looking for experimental vs. established care.

### Key Insight
Academic and clinical sponsors dominate (77%), with industry contributing only 17.5%. Federal funding is surprisingly low at 2.4%, which limits the translational pipeline from clinic to community — particularly in underserved areas.
