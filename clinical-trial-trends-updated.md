## Recommendations

1. **Expand Phase 3 prosthetic trials** — only 30 exist across 644 studies; the evidence base is insufficient for clinical guidelines
2. **Increase industry sponsorship** — 76.5% academic-only research limits scale and generalizability
3. **Reduce UNKNOWN status entries** — 20.8% of studies have unknown status — improve data quality
4. **Establish trial sites in underserved regions** — WV, KY, and MS Delta have zero representation in prosthetic trials
5. **Track completion-to-practice translation** — 42% of trials complete but little evidence of adoption
6. **Leverage the 162 actively-engaged trials** — connect these to underserved populations who are currently excluded
