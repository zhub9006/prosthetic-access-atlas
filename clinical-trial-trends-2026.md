## Recommendations

1. **Expand Phase 3 prosthetic trials** — the current evidence base is insufficient for clinical guidelines
2. **Increase industry sponsorship** — academic-only research limits scale and generalizability
3. **Reduce UNKNOWN status entries** — improve data quality and transparency
4. **Establish trial sites in underserved regions** — WV, KY, and MS Delta have zero representation
5. **Track completion-to-practice translation** — 42% of trials complete but little evidence of adoption
