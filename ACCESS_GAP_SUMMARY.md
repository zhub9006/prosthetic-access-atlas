# Access Gap Summary — Prosthetic Care in Underserved U.S. Regions

## Executive Summary

Three medically underserved regions in the United States have **zero certified prosthetic-orthotic (CPO) providers** within a 100 km radius:

| Region | CPO (50km) | CPO (100km) | Distance to Nearest CPO | Nearest City | Travel Time | Medicaid | Amputation Rate |
|--------|-----------|------------|--------------------------|-------------|-------------|----------|----------------|
| Rural West Virginia | 0 | 0 | ~190 mi | Charleston, WV | 3+ hrs | Not expanded | High |
| Eastern Kentucky | 0 | 0 | ~130 mi | Lexington, KY | 3+ hrs | Not expanded | High |
| Mississippi Delta | 0 | 0 | ~200 mi | Memphis, TN | 4+ hrs | Not expanded | Highest in U.S. |

## Regional Detail

### Rural West Virginia (Beckley, WV)
- **Coordinates:** 37.78°N, 81.19°W
- **Providers within 50 km:** 9 pharmacies, 4 clinics, 1 doctor, 3 dentists, 1 audiology, 1 dialysis, 0 prosthetic/orthotic
- **Providers within 100 km:** Extends to Blacksburg, VA (physiotherapy, occupational therapy)
- **Neighborhood score:** Walkability 0/100, Healthcare 7.9/100
- **Key barrier:** No public transit, WV did not expand Medicaid

### Eastern Kentucky (Pikeville, KY)
- **Coordinates:** 37.48°N, 82.52°W
- **Providers within 50 km:** 6 pharmacies, 4 clinics, 5 doctors, 2 dentists, 1 optometry, 1 audiology, 1 rehabilitation, 0 prosthetic/orthotic
- **Providers within 100 km:** Limited — Logan Foot Clinic (foot care, not CPO)
- **Neighborhood score:** Walkability 4/100, Healthcare 7.6/100
- **Key barrier:** No public transit, KY limited Medicaid coverage

### Mississippi Delta (Yazoo City, MS)
- **Coordinates:** 32.85°N, 90.41°W
- **Providers within 50 km:** 18+ pharmacies, 1 hospital, multiple clinics, 6 dentists, multiple specialists, 0 prosthetic/orthotic
- **Providers within 100 km:** None — prosthetic/orthotic services are entirely absent
- **Neighborhood score:** Walkability 2/100, Healthcare 7.6/100
- **Key barrier:** No public transit, MS did not expand Medicaid, highest amputation rates in U.S.

## Clinical Trial Relevance
- **Zero prosthetic clinical trial sites** in any of the three regions
- **159 U.S. studies** on amputation/prosthetics — none participated by WV, KY, or MS
- **Evidence gap:** Phase 3 trials for prosthetic devices are critically scarce (n=29 globally, n=7 in US)

## Methodology
- Data sources: ClinicalTrials.gov (API v2), OpenStreetMap (OSM MCP tools)
- Analysis date: July 2026
- Search radius: 50 km and 100 km from regional center points
- Provider categories: healthcare (hospital, clinic, pharmacy, doctor), amenity (healthcare, office)
