# Access Gap Analysis — Underserved U.S. Regions

Analysis of prosthetic/orthotic care provider availability in three high-need underserved regions.

## Methodology

- Representative town selected in each region (Beckley WV, Pikeville KY, Greenville MS)
- 30 km-radius search using OpenStreetMap healthcare/amenity categories
- Neighborhood livability scoring (0–10 scale across 10 categories)
- CPO (Certified Prosthetist-Orthotist) proximity measured from representative town center

## Conclusion

All three regions exhibit a **complete absence** of CPO-certified prosthetic/orthotic providers within a 30 km radius. The nearest specialized care requires journeys of 130–200 miles — well beyond the threshold for reasonable access. This gap is structural, not marginal, and compounded in the MS Delta by the near-total collapse of basic services (food, shopping, transport).
