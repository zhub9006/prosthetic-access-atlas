## Broader Observations
- 86 US-based prosthetic studies are actively recruiting or recently active
- 69 studies focus specifically on prosthetic limb amputation
- Industry-sponsored device trials are rare but growing (Össur, Mann Foundation)
- No trials located in WV, KY, or MS despite these regions having the highest amputation rates
