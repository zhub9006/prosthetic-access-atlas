## Notable Active / Recent Trials

### NCT05990062 — Improving Prosthetic Arms for Amputee (KSH System)
- **Sponsor:** VA Office of Research and Development
- **Status:** RECRUITING
- **Phase:** Device Innovation
- **Focus:** Variable-compliance socket/harness system using athletic shoe materials
- **Innovation:** Digital fitting, CNC components, textile-based suspension
- **Enrollment:** 30 Veterans
- **Significance:** Addresses fit/comfort as #1 reason for prosthetic abandonment

### NCT07519746 — Gaza Prosthetic Satisfaction & QoL Study (PROINGA)
- **Sponsor:** Yeditepe University / Al-Azhar University Gaza
- **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Phase:** Observational (patient registry)
- **Enrollment:** 128 participants
- **Focus:** Satisfaction, quality of life, prosthetic use patterns
- **Significance:** Models prosthetics access research in conflict zones

## Gaps Identified

1. **Phase 3 evidence deficit:** Only 30 trials (4.7%) assess comparative efficacy
2. **Unknown status prevalence:** 20.8% of studies have unknown status — many may be abandoned
3. **Industry underrepresentation:** Low commercial investment in prosthetic innovation
4. **Geographic concentration:** Most research in wealthy nations, not in underserved regions
5. **Observational dominance:** 52.5% of studies are registries/observational — limited causal inference
