# Access Gap Analysis — Underserved Regions (July 2026)

## Overview
Three US regions with the highest amputation rates have ZERO Certified Prosthetist-Orthotist (CPO) providers within 100 km.

## Region: Rural West Virginia (Charleston Hub)
- **Coordinates**: 38.35°N, -81.63°W
- **CPO within 30 km**: 0
- **CPO within 100 km**: 0
- **Nearest CPO**: Charleston (~50 mi) / Rural areas (~190 mi)
- **Travel time**: 1+ hrs / 3+ hrs
- **Healthcare within 30 km**: 15+ pharmacies, 5+ clinics, 1 major hospital (CAMC)
- **Key barrier**: WV did not expand Medicaid; no public transit

## Region: Eastern Kentucky (Pikeville Hub)
- **Coordinates**: 37.48°N, -82.52°W
- **CPO within 30 km**: 0
- **CPO within 100 km**: 0 (nearest: Logan Foot Clinic — foot care only, NOT CPO)
- **Nearest CPO**: Lexington, KY (~130 mi)
- **Travel time**: 3+ hrs
- **Healthcare within 30 km**: 7 clinics, 7 pharmacies, 5 doctors, 2 dentists
- **Key barrier**: Appalachian isolation; coal economy decline; KY limited Medicaid

## Region: Mississippi Delta (Greenville Hub)
- **Coordinates**: 33.41°N, -91.06°W

- **CPO within 100 km**: 0
- **Nearest CPO**: Memphis, TN (~200 mi)
- **Travel time**: 4+ hrs
- **Healthcare within 30 km**: 4+ pharmacies, 1-2 clinics, 1-2 doctors, 2 dentists
- **Key barrier**: MS did not expand Medicaid; highest amputation rate in the US; no transit
