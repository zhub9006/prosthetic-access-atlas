## The MS Delta Paradox
The Mississippi Delta has the **highest amputation rate in the United States** (driven by diabetes, peripheral vascular disease, and obesity) yet has the **worst prosthetic/orthotic access** of any region analyzed. The combination of:
- Highest need
- Zero CPO within 100+ km
- No Medicaid expansion
- No public transit
- Limited grocery/health infrastructure

...creates a compounding crisis where patients with the greatest need receive the least care.
