# Key Prosthetic Clinical Studies (ClinicalTrials.gov)

## NCT01756924 — CEM-102 With Rifampin for Prosthetic Joint Infections
- **Sponsor:** Arrevus Inc. (Industry) | **Phase:** Phase 2 | **Status:** TERMINATED
- **Condition:** Prosthetic Joint Infections (Hip, Knee, Infected Spacers)
- **Enrollment:** 14 (actual)
- **Summary:** Oral CEM-102 (fusidic acid) + rifampin vs. IV/oral standard-of-care antibiotics for PJI/spacer infections. Terminated due to alternative study design considerations.

## NCT03726918 — BioPreIOA (Osseointegration Bio-Markers)
- **Sponsor:** Istituto Ortopedico Rizzoli (Academic) | **Phase:** Observational | **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Condition:** Osteoarthritis (prosthetic implant context)
- **Enrollment:** 9 (actual)
- **Summary:** Omics-based analysis of synoviocytes, osteoblasts, and chondrocytes from prosthetic implant tissue. Identifies predictive miRNA/lncRNA biomarker signatures for osseointegration success.
