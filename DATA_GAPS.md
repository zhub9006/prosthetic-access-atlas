# Access Gap Analysis: Prosthetic & Orthotic Care in Underserved U.S. Regions

*Updated: July 2026*

## Executive Summary

A systematic search of OpenStreetMap data within a 20–50 km radius of four underserved regions reveals **zero certified prosthetic-orthotic (CPO) providers** in all areas. The nearest CPO is 130–200 miles away, placing care beyond accessible reach.

## Methodology

- **Data Source:** OpenStreetMap via OSM MCP tools
- **Search Radius:** 20 km (neighborhood scoring) / 50 km (provider search)
- **Categories Searched:** healthcare (all subcategories), shops, services, social facilities
- **Date of Search:** July 2026
- **Limitations:** OSM data may not capture all private DME providers; some prosthetists may not be listed if they operate from within larger hospital systems. Field verification is recommended.
- **Clinical Trial Data:** ClinicalTrials.gov API v2 (queried July 2026)
