## Trials Not Yet in Registry

Based on the gap analysis, there is a critical shortage of clinical trials focused on:
1. **Prosthetic access in rural/underserved areas** — Almost 0 trials locate in Appalachia, Delta, or rural WV/KY
2. **Teleprosthetics / telesocket fitting** — Very few trials test remote prosthetic adjustment methods
3. **Low-cost prosthetics for resource-limited settings** — Most trials concentrate in wealthy nations
4. **Prosthetic abandonment in underserved populations** — Unknown prevalence; no dedicated trials
5. **Mobile O&P clinic effectiveness** — No trials validating mobile prosthetic care models
