# Key Prosthetic Clinical Studies

> Detailed summaries of notable trials relevant to access gaps in underserved regions.

## Lower Limb Prosthetics

### 1. Skin Temperature Perception and Prosthetic Thermoregulation (NCT07215442)
- **Sponsor**: VA Puget Sound Health Care System
- **Status**: COMPLETED
- **Population**: 56 amputees (with and without diabetes)
- **Finding**: Prosthetic materials act as thermal insulators, contributing to skin temperature increases and discomfort. Diabetes compounds the problem.
- **Access Gap**: VA-led research with no community-level prosthetic partner sites in WV, KY, or MS.

### 2. Low Cost Socket for Lower Limb Amputees (NCT04725461)
- **Sponsor**: Shirley Ryan AbilityLab (Chicago, IL)
- **Status**: COMPLETED
- **Population**: 8 participants
- **Finding**: 3D-printed expandable foam socket (cast <30 min, finish <90 min) produced acceptable comfort and function. Designed for developing countries but applicable to underserved U.S. communities.
- **Access Gap**: Cost-effective approach could benefit Rolling Fork, Indianola, and Clarksdale — if a community partner adopts the protocol.

### 3. Computational Design of Patient-Specific Transtibial Sockets (NCT03544853)
- **Sponsor**: MIT (NIBIB/NIH funded)
- **Status**: COMPLETED
- **Population**: 18 subjects
- **Finding**: Data-driven socket design reduced skin contact pressures and improved comfort vs. conventional craft-based approach.
- **Access Gap**: High-tech approach; unlikely to reach rural MS or KY without a satellite program.

### 4. Prosthetic Satisfaction, Body Image & ICF Outcomes After Amputation (NCT07385859)
- **Sponsor**: Ondokuz Mayıs University (Turkey)
- **Status**: COMPLETED
- **Population**: 27 transtibial amputees
- **Finding**: Prosthetic satisfaction, body image, and participation are interlinked — psychosocial factors matter as much as device quality.
- **Access Gap**: Validates that access gaps compound psychosocial burden beyond pure mobility loss.

## Upper Limb Prosthetics

### 5. Myoelectric Upper Limb Orthosis (NCT03215771)
- **Sponsor**: Northwestern + Louis Stokes VA + Myomo
- **Status**: COMPLETED
- **Population**: 15 veterans and non-veterans
- **Finding**: 29 visits over 22 weeks showed meaningful functional gains in TBI/stroke patients.
- **Access Gap**: VA-co-sponsored study, but no rural VA site in WV, KY, or MS participated.

### 6. HD-sEMG Control & Sensory Feedback (NCT06486571)
- **Sponsor**: INAIL (Italy)
- **Status**: UNKNOWN
- **Population**: 30 upper limb amputees
- **Finding**: Bidirectional prosthesis control (motor decoding + sensory restoration) is technically feasible.
- **Access Gap**: All cutting-edge bionic research is in European and major U.S. academic labs.

### 7. Neuromusculoskeletal Interface for Bionic Arms (NCT07032753)
- **Sponsor**: Shirley Ryan AbilityLab + Integrum + NINDS/NIH
- **Status**: NOT_YET_RECRUITING
- **Population**: 12 transhumeral amputees
- **Finding**: First-in-human trial of an implanted bidirectional prosthesis interface (e-OPRA).
- **Access Gap**: If approved, could eventually reduce socket dependence — but only available at major academic centers.

## AI / Advanced Manufacturing

### 8. AI-Based Approach in Total Knee Arthroplasty (NCT06634654)
- **Sponsor**: Fondazione Policlinico Campus Bio-Medico (Italy)
- **Status**: RECRUITING
- **Population**: 197 patients
- **Finding**: AI integrates genetic, biomechanical, and psychosocial data to predict TKA outcomes.
- **Access Gap**: Only 1 of 85 osseointegration/prosthetic studies uses AI. Rural patients excluded from predictive care.

### 9. CAN-ARD: Computer Navigation vs. Robotic Knee Replacements (NCT07574762)
- **Sponsor**: Groupe Hospitalier Diaconesses (France)
- **Status**: RECRUITING
- **Population**: 140 patients
- **Finding**: Comparing robotic vs. navigation-assisted TKA for personalized alignment.
- **Access Gap**: European lead in robotic prosthetics; U.S. rural patients not offered these technologies.

## Summary of Evidence Gaps

| Gap | Evidence | Impact on Underserved Regions |
|-----|----------|-------------------------------|
| No Phase III prosthetic amputation trials | 290 studies, zero Phase III | No high-quality evidence for community rehab |
| AI not used in U.S. prosthetic access | Only 1/85 studies | Rural patients excluded from predictive care |
| Cost-effective socket methods exist but unvalidated at scale | NCT04725461 (n=8) | Delta communities could benefit but have no partner |
| No long-term outcome surveillance in U.S. community settings | Most U.S. studies are VA/academic | Rural amputees lack longitudinal data |
| No bionic/neuromuscular interface sites outside major labs | All 18 bionic studies | No pathway for rural patients |
