## Summary

The prosthetic clinical trial ecosystem is:
- **Mature** (42% completed) but **slowly recruiting** (17.5% active)
- **Observation-heavy** (52.5% are non-interventional)
- **Geographically concentrated** in the U.S. and Western Europe
- **Underrepresented** in Africa, Asia, the Middle East, and rural portions of the U.S.

This creates a dual challenge: limited research in underserved regions AND limited access to prosthetic technologies in those regions.
