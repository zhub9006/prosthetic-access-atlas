# Access Disparity Map

> Visual representation of prosthetic care access across the United States.

## Coast vs. Interior
```
Coastal Cities (Boston, NYC, SF, LA):
  30-50+ prosthetists within 25km
  Academic centers with prosthetics R&D
  Multiple O&P clinics in each metro area

Mountain/Plains States:
  1-5 providers within 250km
  Long distances between centers
  No specialized prosthetic rehabilitation

Appalachia & Delta:
  ZERO providers within 100km
  Amputation rates 2-4x national average
  Highest diabetes prevalence in the nation
```
