# Eastern Kentucky: Prosthetic/Orthotic Access Desert

## Geography

Eastern Kentucky is part of the Appalachian coalfields region with high disability rates, economic distress, and healthcare scarcity. Anchor cities:

- **Pikeville, KY** (37.48, -82.52) — Pike County seat, population ~7,000
- **Ashland, KY** (38.48, -82.64) — Boyd County seat, population ~21,000 (smaller metro hop to Charleston/WV/Ohio)

## Findings

### No CPOs in Eastern Kentucky

The Eastern Kentucky counties surrounding Pikeville and Ashland show **complete absence** of prosthetic/orthotic care providers in all categories (health, shop/medical supply). Only churches, cemeteries, schools, automobile-related businesses, and general retail are OSM-listed.

## Cross-Boundary Corridor

Eastern Kentucky patients face a **dual-state barrier**:
- North → Huntington, WV → Pittsburgh (3 hrs + 2 hrs = 5+ hrs total)
- South → Ashland → Covington/Lexington (2.5 hrs multi-state)
- No direct in-state option —Kentucky's entire CPO network is concentrated in Louisville and Lexington

## Relevant Clinical Trials (All Out-of-State)
