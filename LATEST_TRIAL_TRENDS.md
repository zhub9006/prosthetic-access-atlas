## Key Findings

1. **Only 17.4% of all prosthetic studies are actively recruiting** — the pipeline is thin.
2. **Phase 3 efficacy trials are critically scarce** — ~30 globally (4.7%).
3. **76.5% academically sponsored** — limited commercial translation.
4. **52.5% observational/registries** — limited device R&D.
5. **Zero prosthetic trials in WV, KY, or MS** despite high amputation rates.
6. **WD Delta: highest amputation rate in the U.S.** — yet only 1 completed fitted-prosthetic study in 10+ years.
