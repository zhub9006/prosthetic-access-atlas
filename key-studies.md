# Key Clinical Trials — Featured Studies in Prosthetic Care Access

## Study 1: NCT06539936 — Glide Control Strategy (RECRUITING)
**Sponsor:** Infinite Biomedical Technologies + NICHD + Advanced Arm Dynamics
**Enrollment:** 7 (estimated) | **Phase:** N/A | **DMC:** Yes

- Novel myoelectric control using vector summation of EMG channels
- Core2 controller, FlexCell batteries, Glide iPad app
- 24-week AB multiple baseline protocol
- Outcomes: CAPROQ, tBBT, FIT, DASH, SAM
- **Gap:** Sites ONLY at 6 metro Centers of Excellence; no rural/underserved sites
- **Links:** https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT06539936

---

## Study 2: NCT03673371 — Women's-Specific Footwear (COMPLETED)
**Sponsor:** Minneapolis VA Medical Center + DoD
**Enrollment:** 100 | **Phase:** N/A | **Data Sharing:** Yes (PubMed Central)

- First study addressing footwear challenges for women prosthesis users
- Surveyed 60 women with LLA/TKA/TF about limitations
- Data publicly available for reuse
- **Gap:** No rural-specific subgroup analysis
- **Links:** https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT03673371

## Recommendations for Researchers
1. Include rural sites in prosthetic trials
2. Add access measures (distance-to-provider, Medicaid, travel burden)
3. IPD sharing for both NCT trials
4. Community-Based Participatory Research (CBPR)
5. Industry-academic partnerships with rural access commitments
