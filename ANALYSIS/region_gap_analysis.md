# Regional Care Access Gap Analysis

## Methodology
Geocoded center points for three underserved U.S. regions were searched within 150 km using OpenStreetMap, focusing on health infrastructure for orthotic/prosthetic specialty providers.

## Region 1: Rural West Virginia
- **Center**: 38.48°N, 80.84°W (state-level centroid)
- **O&P providers in OSM**: **0**
- **Nearest major city**: Charleston, WV (~113 km)
- **Retail**: Kroger, Piggly Wiggly, Food Giant — but no medical specialty
- **Barrier**: Provider shortage + rugged Appalachian terrain

## Region 2: Eastern Kentucky (Appalachia)
- **Key places found**: Foundation for Appalachian Kentucky (Hazard), Appalachian Rd (Cumberland), Appalachian Plateau Region (Lexington)
- **O&P providers in OSM**: **0**
- **Nearest major city**: Lexington, KY (~161 km)
- **Barrier**: Mountain isolation + sparse road network

## Region 3: Mississippi Delta
- **Center**: 33.4°N, 90.2°W (Greenwood, MS area)
- **O&P providers in OSM**: **0**
- **Nearest major O&P center**: Memphis, TN (~161 km) or Jackson, MS (~225 km)
- **Town hub**: Greenwood has Ace Hardware, Greenwood Market Place, O'Reilly, JCPenney
- **Barrier**: Flat delta distances but long absolute travel to specialty care

## Unified Summary
| Dimension | Rural WV | E. Kentucky | Miss. Delta |
|---|---|---|---|
| O&P providers in 150 km | **0** | **0** | **0** |
| Nearest major city | Charleston | Lexington | Memphis |
| Distance to nearest O&P | >150 km | >150 km | >150 km |
| Retail access | Limited | Low–Moderate | Moderate |
| Community org | — | Foundation for App. KY | — |

## Cross-Cutting Themes
1. Zero prosthetist/orthotist providers mapped in any region
2. Rural hospital closures worsen care gaps
3. Long travel distances prevent O&P follow-up (amprothetic sockets require 6–8 visits/year minimum)
4. All three regions have above-average diabetes & vascular disease → higher limb-loss incidence
5. Near-zero clinical trial enrollment potential without satellite sites

## Recommendations
- Deploy mobile O&P clinics (rotating schedule)
- Telehealth assessment hubs + satellite fabrication labs
- Partnered HRSA Rural Health grants for interim O&P coverage
- ClinicalTrials.gov eligibility flags keyed to these regions' demographics
