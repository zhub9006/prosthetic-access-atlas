- More studies have terminated or been withdrawn (12.6%) than are currently recruiting (17.6%)

### Key Insight
- **Academic/other sponsors dominate (76.8%)** — strong research base, but limited commercial translation
- **Industry sponsors account for only 17.5%** — low private-sector investment in prosthetic innovation
- **Federal investigators are underrepresented (2.4–5.8%)** — suggests potential for federal-funding-driven outreach in underserved areas
- **No NIH-led industry partnerships** in the dataset — opportunity for public-private partnership
